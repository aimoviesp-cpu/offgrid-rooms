Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about regulations, power, and mobility.

Do I need permits?

In many areas, container-based temporary structures under certain sizes are treated differently from permanent buildings. Regulations vary by city and county. We help you understand local rules and provide documentation for your unit if needed.

How long does installation take?

Once your OffGrid Room arrives, placement usually takes a few hours. Because the unit is pre-wired and pre-configured, you can typically start using it the same day.

How much power do the solar panels provide?

We size each system according to your intended use (office, living, rental) and typical weather conditions in your area, ensuring sufficient power for lighting, outlets, and climate control under normal use.

Can I move the unit later?

Yes. OffGrid Rooms are designed to be relocatable. They can be moved between sites with a truck and the appropriate lifting equipment.

Do you offer customization?

We offer multiple layouts, finishes, and furnishing packages. For fleet or park orders, we can also add your company branding and custom configurations.
